Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Irving Industries LLC collects, uses, stores, and protects information in connection with its websites, mobile applications, digital products, software services, and related communications.

1. Information We May Collect

Irving Industries LLC may collect information that users voluntarily provide, including names, email addresses, account information, support requests, business inquiries, and other information submitted through forms, apps, or direct communication.

Our applications may also collect limited technical information such as device type, app usage data, crash diagnostics, performance data, subscription status, and general analytics used to improve reliability and user experience.

2. How Information Is Used

Information may be used to operate and improve our products, provide app functionality, respond to inquiries, support users, process subscriptions, improve safety and security, analyze performance, and communicate product updates or business opportunities.

3. App Store, Payments, and Third-Party Platforms

Payments, subscriptions, and downloads may be handled through Apple App Store services or other authorized third-party platforms. Irving Industries LLC does not directly collect or store full payment card information from App Store transactions.

4. Data Sharing

Irving Industries LLC does not sell personal information. Information may be shared only as needed with trusted service providers, analytics platforms, hosting providers, legal advisors, or as required by law, regulation, legal process, or business protection needs.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect information from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure. No digital system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

6. User Choices

Users may contact Irving Industries LLC to request access, correction, or deletion of certain information, subject to legal, technical, and operational limitations.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our products are not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under the age required by applicable law. If we become aware that such information has been collected, we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

8. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy may be sent to irvingindustries@outlook.com.
